🔄 Workflow
How PaperMentor processes your exam papers
1
Upload Past Papers

Add 1–10+ question papers from multiple years. Each file is parsed for questions, topics, marks, and patterns. Supports any subject or domain.

2
AI Topic Extraction

Groq AI reads all papers and extracts topics, question types, difficulty levels, marks distribution, and phrasing patterns in real-time.

3
Syllabus Mapping

Extracted topics are cross-referenced against your syllabus. Coverage percentages and gaps are identified. Topics never tested are flagged as surprise risks.

4
Priority Scoring

Each topic receives an importance score based on frequency, recency of appearance, marks weight, and difficulty trend. Topics are ranked High/Medium/Low.

5
Insights & Study Plan

AI ranks topics, predicts exam likelihood, generates a personalized study schedule, creates practice questions, and provides an AI assistant for on-demand help.

💡 Tips & Tricks
Get the most out of PaperMentor
📅 More years = better predictions

Upload at least 5 years of papers for meaningful trend analysis. 10+ years gives exceptional prediction accuracy. Label each file with its year.

📋 Include your full syllabus

Paste all official syllabus topics for complete gap analysis. Even topics that never appear in papers are valuable — they might appear this year as surprises.

✨ Use the AI Assistant actively

After analysis, ask the AI for a crash course plan, ask about specific topics, or get explanations for difficult concepts. The assistant has full context of your analysis.

🎯 Focus on High Priority topics first

High-priority topics typically cover 60–70% of exam marks. Spend 60% of study time there, 30% on medium, 10% on low priority and coverage gaps.

🔄 Re-analyze after adding papers

When you get a new past paper, add it and re-analyze. The AI recalculates all scores and predictions with the updated dataset.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about PaperMentor
Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All file processing happens in your browser. Files are read locally. Paper content is sent to Groq AI for analysis, but nothing is stored permanently.

What file types are supported?

PDF, PNG, JPG, JPEG, and TXT files. For best results, use clear scans or digital PDFs. Handwritten papers work but with reduced accuracy.

What subjects/domains does it support?

Any domain: Engineering, Medicine, Law, Commerce, Arts, Sciences, Languages, Competitive exams (UPSC, JEE, NEET, GATE, CAT, BAR), university exams, school exams — if it's a written exam, PaperMentor can analyze it.

How many papers should I upload?

Minimum 1 paper (basic analysis), ideal 3–5 years (good trends), excellent 7–10+ years (highly accurate predictions).

Why is the AI analysis taking time?

Groq AI reads all your papers and generates a comprehensive JSON analysis. For 5+ papers, this typically takes 20–60 seconds. Larger files take longer.
